IATI identifier checks

There are checks for activity identifiers and for organisation identifiers.

The organisation identifiers can occur both in activity files and organisation files.

For each element or attribute, the tests are performed going left to right.

So an occurrence of an identifier may lead to multiple feedback messages. For example: a reporting organisation that does not yet occur in the list downloaded from the registry may have three messages:

  • A warning about leading or trailing whitespace.
  • A warning about forbidden symbols or characters in the identifier.
  • A warning there is no known identifier prefix that matches the identifier.
